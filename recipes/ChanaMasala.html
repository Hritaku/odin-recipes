Chana Masala

Description

Chana Masala is a delicious North Indian chickpea curry made with tender chickpeas simmered in a flavorful tomato-based sauce. This protein-rich dish is easy to make and pairs wonderfully with rice, roti, or naan.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas (or 2 cups canned chickpeas, drained and rinsed)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Soak dried chickpeas overnight, then boil them until soft. If using canned chickpeas, rinse and drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Once they crackle, add chopped onions and saut√© until golden.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, and tomatoes. Cook until the tomatoes soften.
  4. Stir in turmeric, coriander, chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add the chickpeas and mix to coat with spices. Pour in 1 cup of water and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Stir in garam masala and let it cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or roti.
